Potato Soup

Let's cook tasty

  1. Heat a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Melt butter.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.
  4. Add diced potatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Pour in chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
  6. Using an immersion blender or transferring batches to a blender, blend the soup until smooth or desired consistency is reached. You can also mash the potatoes with a potato masher for a more chunky texture.
  7. Return the soup to the pot and stir in milk. Add heavy cream, if using. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

Ingredients required*

  1. 2 tablespoons Butter (salted or unsalted)
  2. 1 medium Onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  4. 3-4 medium Russet Potatoes, peeled and diced
  5. 4 cups Chicken Broth or Vegetable Broth
  6. 1 1/2 cups Milk (whole milk or low-fat milk)
  7. 1/2 cup Heavy Cream (optional, for extra richness)
  8. 1/2 teaspoon Salt (or to taste)
  9. 1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper (or to taste)
