Let's cook tasty

  1. Heat a saucepan and melt 2 tablespoons of butter (salted or unsalted).
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of whole wheat flour (or 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour) and stir quickly to avoid lumps.
  3. Saut√© until the flour is pale golden and has a nice cooked aroma.
  4. Pour in 1 cup of cold or chilled milk in a gentle stream while stirring the sauce mixture.
  5. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.
  6. Toss the cooked penne pasta in the white sauce and serve warm

Ingredients required*

  1. Penne Pasta (or any other type of pasta)
  2. Mixed Vegetables (such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, sweet corn, baby corn, mushrooms, broccoli, zucchini)
  3. Ripe Tomatoes (fresh or tomato puree like passata)
  4. Indian Spices (cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, red chili powder, garam masala, kasuri methi)
  5. Olive Oil
  6. Salt
  7. Fresh Coriander Leaves
