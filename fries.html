Let's cook tasty

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large saucepan to 325°F (163°C).
  2. **Optional (for crispier fries):** Soak cut fries in cold water for 30 minutes to 1 hour. Drain and pat thoroughly dry with a clean kitchen towel.
  3. Fry a small batch of fries at a time (not overcrowding the pan) for 2-3 minutes, or until lightly golden brown. Remove with a slotted spoon and transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  4. **Double fry (for extra crispy fries):** Increase oil temperature to 375°F (190°C) and fry the pre-cooked fries for another 1-2 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Season with salt immediately after removing from the oil. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauces.

Ingredients required*

  1. 2-3 Russet potatoes (or starchy potatoes), peeled and cut into sticks (about ½ inch thick)
  2. Vegetable oil (enough for frying)
  3. Salt (to taste)
  4. Optional: Seasonings of your choice (e.g., paprika, garlic powder, dried herbs)
